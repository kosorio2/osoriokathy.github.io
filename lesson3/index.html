Site Plan

Site Name: Elemental Forecast

Domain Name: elementalforecast.com
Cost of domain: $12 a month

Purpose

The purpose of elemetalforecast.com is to provide an updated weather report to those residing in Easter Idaho. The site will allow those who visit it to view the weather not only for that specific day, but it will also give them a weekly forecast. The site will also provide a storm watch alert which will allow the viewers to stay up to date on the most recent storms in the area.

History of Conzumel Island

Conzumel is an island located in the Caribbean Sea, SouthEast of Mexico. It is Mexico's Largest inhabited islands. Conzumel Island is a popular tourist destination. It has beautiful coral reefs, an abundant amount of marine life, clear waters. It is also one of the best scuba-diving destinations. Cruise ships are constanlty docking bringing new people around.

Locations To Visit and Cozumel Island Features

Conzumel Beaches

Stop by and visit one of our 6 famous beaches.
They are the perfect locations for tanning,
spending time with kids or just relaxing
with a cocunut in hand.

Swim With The Turtles

If swimming with Sea Turles has been on
your bucket list, then you came to right place.
Thanks to the clear waters, those visiting Conzumel Island
can swim with our beautiful turtles.

Palancar Reef

Come and visit one of the best snorkling locations.
Dive down and visit the large bronze figure of Christ
located about 17 meters underwater.

Cozumel's National Marine Park

Laguna Chankanaab is our famous fresh water lake.
Visitors can choose between swimming in its clear waters,
or visiting the Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park.
No matter what you choose, we know you will have fun.

Rio Secreto

Rio Secreto contains miles of cavernous underground
passages. Stop by and learn about its great Mayan history.
Take part in one of our various activities, such as,
swimming through crystal clear water,
and taking pictures of the marvelous limestone.

Stingray Beach

Are you a brave and adventurous person?
If your answer is yes, then you will love Stingray Beach!
The name says it all! Rent some scuba or snorkling gear
and swim with these mysterious marine creatures.
